What is cozy Homes'

Our mission is to empower consumers with information and tools to
make smart decisions about homes and real estate.


Where does we get information from?

Cozy Homes' receives information about property sales from the municipal office responsible for recording real estate transactions in your area.
The information we provide is public information, gathered from county records.
Our index information, which outlines the lot on which your house sits, comes from various public sources, such as the county.
We also use Chicago data portal to fing the livability index and other life style indexes.
We regularly update the information as we receive it from data providers.
